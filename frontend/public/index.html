Frequently Asked Questions

Find Questions to common questions and answers.

What is Film Remedy?

Film Remedy is an innovative app that allows you to identify movies by simply scanning a scene with your device’s camera. It provides instant access to movie titles, release dates, and detailed descriptions.

How do I use Film Remedy?

Using Film Remedy is easy! Just open the app, sign in, and use the camera feature to scan a scene from any movie. Film Remedy will instantly recognize the movie and provide you with all the relevant information.

Do I need an account to use Film Remedy?

Yes, you need to sign in to use Film Remedy’s features. This allows us to provide a personalized experience and save your favorite movies and scanning history.

What information does Film Remedy provide?

Film Remedy provides comprehensive information about the movie, including the title, release date, synopsis, cast, and more

Is Film Remedy free to use?

Film Remedy offers both free and premium subscription plans. The free plan provides basic features, while the premium plans offer additional benefits and features. You can choose the plan that best fits your needs.

How do I subscribe to a premium plan?

You can subscribe to a premium plan directly within the app. We offer easy PayPal integration for a seamless subscription process

Can I save movies to my library?

Yes, you can add movies to your personal collection and easily revisit them anytime. Your library is always at your fingertips

What devices are compatible?

Film Remedy is compatible with most modern smartphones and tablets that have a camera and internet access.

How do I contact support if I have an issue?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our support team through the app or by visiting our website’s support section

Is my data safe with Film Remedy?

Yes, we take your privacy and data security seriously. All your data is securely stored and handled in accordance with our privacy policy.
